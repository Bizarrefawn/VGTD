Biography of Michael Caissie
Michael Caissie, age 18, birthday December 24th 1999. Born in the Queen Elizabeth Hospital (Q.E.H) in Halifax, Nova Scotia. started early child schooling at Precious Lambs Daycare then proceeded on to the first three years of grade school at Greenfield Elementry. after that I moved on to the next 3 years at Elmstreet Elementry. within the following 3 years I moved up to Summerside Intermediate School, and for my last three years they were spent geetting a high school diploma from Three Oaks Senior High School. My life has gonna in many ways it had its ups and downs and it has landed me here at Eastern College, pursuing my dream job, Quality Advisor or QA as some would call it. Growing up i had always had an easy supply of work with being neighbors to an elderly farm couple. I would plant and pick vegetables and load/unload logs into their basement. As I grew up it was time to start earning more so i got a real job at an amusment park, Burlington Amusment Park. After that i got a job at Prince Edward Aqua Farms. With the money I saved i moved away to Moncton, New Brunswick with my girlfriend we rented an apartment. That about sums up my life in a nutshell and hopefully you learnd a little something about me.
